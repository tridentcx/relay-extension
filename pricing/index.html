Built for trust, not attention.

Relay is deliberately small: a private vault, one clear sync path, and controls that stay quiet until bookmarks need updating.

Can Relay read my bookmarks?
No. Bookmarks are encrypted on your device before sync. The server stores an unreadable vault.
What happens if I cancel?
You return to Free. Nothing is deleted, but Pro-only limits and features stop applying.
Why upgrade from the extension?
Relay avoids email accounts. Starting from the extension lets Pro attach to the correct vault cleanly.
What happens when bookmarks change?
Relay compares your browser with the encrypted vault so changes move with you instead of being repeated by hand.
