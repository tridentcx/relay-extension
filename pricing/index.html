Questions

Can Relay read my bookmarks?
No. Your bookmarks are encrypted with your password before they leave your device. We store an unreadable blob. Not even we can see them.
What happens if I cancel?
You go back to the free plan immediately. Your bookmarks stay safe and synced — you just lose the Pro features. Nothing is deleted.
Why do you need my username to upgrade?
Relay has no email accounts — just a username and password. We hash your username to link the Pro subscription to your vault anonymously. We never store your actual username.
What is sync history?
Every time you sync, we keep a snapshot. If you accidentally delete bookmarks, you can restore from any point in the last 30 days.
Does this work on Safari?
Yes — Safari requires a one-time Xcode build (free), after which it works identically to Chrome and Edge. Instructions are in the extension.
