How Relay protects you
👤Username
Hashed with SHA-256 to locate your vault. Never stored in plaintext — the database only sees a 64-char hash. Nobody can reverse it to find your username.
🔑Password
Encrypts your bookmarks using AES-256-GCM with PBKDF2 key derivation (310,000 iterations). Never stored or transmitted — only you know it.
☁️What's in the cloud
Only a SHA-256 username hash and an encrypted blob. Neither reveals who you are or what your bookmarks are.
