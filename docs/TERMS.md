## Service

Relay provides encrypted bookmark sync for supported browsers. The service stores encrypted bookmark vaults and minimal operational metadata needed for sync, billing, browser limits, abuse prevention, and account deletion.

## Accounts

Relay accounts use a username and password. Relay does not provide password recovery. If you lose your password, Relay cannot decrypt or recover your bookmark vault.

You are responsible for keeping your password and browser profile secure.

## Plans and billing

Relay may offer Free and Pro plans. Pro billing is handled by Stripe. Stripe is responsible for payment processing and card data. Relay does not store card numbers.

Plan limits may be enforced by browser count, bookmark count, sync history access, and other product controls described in the extension or pricing page.

## Privacy and security

Relay is designed so bookmark contents are encrypted before upload. Relay cannot read bookmark contents without the user's password. See [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md) and [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

No system is perfect. Users should keep independent browser or system backups for important bookmark data.
